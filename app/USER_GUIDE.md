## Introduction

### What is Human Rights DAO?

Human Rights DAO (HR DAO) is a decentralized autonomous organization platform that incentivizes civic engagement through blockchain-based token rewards. Built on the Cardano blockchain, the platform allows users to:

- **Earn tokens** by completing activism tasks (petitions, sharing content, attending events)
- **Support campaigns** by donating earned tokens to human rights causes
- **Connect with the community** through real-time chat channels
- **Track your impact** through detailed transaction history and statistics

### Key Features

- **Non-custodial wallet**: Your wallet is stored securely on your device
- **Blockchain-based rewards**: All token transactions are recorded on the Cardano blockchain
- **Multi-platform support**: Available on iOS, Android, and desktop
- **Real-time updates**: Live chat and instant transaction confirmations
- **Multiple languages**: Support for English, Traditional Chinese, and Thai

## Getting Started

### Registration

1. **Launch the Application**
   - Open the HR DAO app on your device
   - You'll see the splash screen with the option to select your language

2. **Create Your Account**
   - Tap "Get Started" to navigate to the registration page
   - Fill in the registration form:
     - **Display Name** (required): How you want to be known in the community
     - **Email** (optional): For campaign updates
     - **Profile Image** (optional): Upload a profile picture
     - **Country** (required): Select your country from the dropdown
     - **Areas of Interest** (optional): Select human rights causes you care about:
       - Freedom of Expression
       - Environmental Rights
       - Children's Rights
       - Refugee Rights
       - Women's Rights
       - LGBTQ+ Rights
       - Digital Rights
       - Economic Justice
     - **Terms of Service**: Read and accept the terms to continue

3. **Account and Wallet Creation**
   - After registration, your profile and wallet will be created on your phone.
   - You'll receive 10 HR Tokens as a registration reward
   **Important**: If you uninstall the HRDAO you will lose access to your wallet and your tokens.

4. **Automatic Login**
   - If you've previously registered, the app will attempt to log you in automatically
   - Your wallet credentials are stored securely on your device

## Contributions & Earning Tokens

### What are Contributions?

Contributions are activism tasks you can complete to earn HR Tokens. Each contribution has:
- A title and description
- A token reward amount
- A type (petition, article, event, action, visit, share, scan)
- Optional external links or QR codes

### Types of Contributions

1. **Visit** 👁️
   - View content or external links
   - Simply click to open and view

2. **Share** 📤
   - Share campaigns and content
   - Uses your device's native share functionality

3. **Scan** 📷
   - Scan QR codes for event check-ins
   - Requires camera permissions

### How to Complete a Contribution

1. **Browse Contributions**
   - Navigate to the "Contributions" or "Actions" section
   - View available contributions in a list or grid
   - Filter by type or search for specific contributions

2. **View Contribution Details**
   - Tap on a contribution to see full details:
     - Title and description
     - Token reward amount
     - Type and category
     - External links (if any)
     - Participant count
     - Completion status

3. **Complete the Contribution**
   - Tap the "Complete Action" button
   - Follow the specific instructions based on type:
     - **Visit**: Opens external link automatically
     - **Share**: Opens share menu - share to complete
     - **Scan**: Opens camera - scan the QR code
     - **Other types**: May require specific actions

4. **Receive Your Reward**
   - Upon successful completion, you'll receive a notification
   - Tokens are automatically added to your wallet
   - The transaction is recorded on the Cardano blockchain
   - You can only complete each contribution once

### Tips for Earning Tokens

- Check regularly for new contributions
- Complete contributions promptly before deadlines
- Share contributions to help spread awareness
- Attend events to earn scan-type contributions
- Read articles and sign petitions to maximize your token earnings

## Campaigns

### What are Campaigns?

Campaigns are fundraising initiatives for human rights causes. You can support campaigns by donating your earned HR Tokens.

### Campaign Categories

- Freedom of Expression
- Environmental Rights
- Children's Rights
- Refugee Rights
- Women's Rights
- LGBTQ+ Rights
- Digital Rights
- Economic Justice

### Viewing Campaigns

1. **Browse Campaigns**
   - Navigate to the "Campaigns" section
   - View all active campaigns
   - See progress bars showing tokens raised vs. goals

2. **Campaign Details**
   - Tap a campaign to view full details:
     - Campaign image and description
     - Category tag
     - Progress: tokens raised / goal
     - Days remaining (if applicable)
     - Supporter count
     - Full campaign details
     - External campaign URL (if available)

### Donating to Campaigns

1. **Select a Campaign**
   - Choose a campaign you want to support
   - Review the campaign details and goals

2. **Make a Donation**
   - Tap "Support Campaign" or "Donate"
   - Enter the amount of HR Tokens you want to donate
   - Review your current balance
   - Confirm the donation

3. **Transaction Process**
   - The app will build a blockchain transaction
   - You'll need to sign the transaction with your wallet
   - The transaction is submitted to the Cardano network
   - You'll see a pending status while it confirms
   - Once confirmed, your donation is recorded

4. **Track Your Donations**
   - View all your donations in your wallet transaction history
   - See the impact of your contributions
   - Campaign progress updates in real-time

### Campaign Features

- **Progress Tracking**: Real-time updates on tokens raised
- **Deadlines**: Some campaigns have time limits
- **Supporter Count**: See how many people have supported
- **External Links**: Learn more about the cause
- **Categories**: See campaigns by human rights category

## Wallet

### Wallet Overview

Your wallet is a non-custodial Cardano wallet that stores your HR Tokens. All transactions are recorded on the Cardano blockchain.

### Viewing Your Wallet

1. **Access Wallet**
   - Navigate to the "Wallet" section
   - View your current token balance at the top

2. **Balance Information**
   - **Your Balance**: Total HR Tokens in your wallet
   - **Treasury Balance**: Total tokens in the DAO treasury (admin view only)
   - Balance updates automatically after transactions

### Transaction History

1. **View Transactions**
   - Scroll down to see your transaction history
   - Transactions are listed chronologically (newest first)

2. **Transaction Types**

   - **REWARD** 🎁
     - Tokens earned from completing contributions
     - Shows contribution title
     - Green/positive indicator

   - **DONATION** ❤️
     - Tokens donated to campaigns
     - Shows campaign name
     - May show campaign category

### Refreshing Your Wallet

- Tap the refresh button to refresh (mobile)
- Balance and transactions update automatically after new transactions

### Wallet Security

- Your wallet is stored securely on your device
- Private keys are never transmitted to servers

## Chat

### Chat Overview

The chat system allows you to communicate with other community members through public channels and private messages.

### Chat Types

1. **Public Channels**
   - Community-wide discussion channels
   - Created and managed by administrators
   - All approved users can participate

2. **Private Chats**
   - Direct messaging between users
   - One-on-one conversations
   - Created when you message another user

### Using Chat

1. **Access Chat**
   - Navigate to the "Chat" section
   - View list of available chats
   - See unread message indicators

2. **Open a Chat**
   - Tap a chat to open the conversation
   - View message history
   - See participant names and profile images

3. **Send Messages**
   - Type your message in the input field
   - Tap send button or press Enter
   - Messages are sent in real-time
   - See read receipts (when messages are read)

4. **Message Features**
   - **Real-time Updates**: Messages appear instantly
   - **Read Receipts**: See when messages are read
   - **Profile Images**: See who you're chatting with
   - **Timestamps**: Messages show when they were sent
   - **Date Dividers**: Automatic date separators for messages

5. **Chat List**
   - See all your chats in one place
   - Unread message counts
   - Last message preview
   - Chat names and participants

### Chat Best Practices

- Be respectful and follow community guidelines
- Use appropriate language
- Stay on topic in public channels
- Report inappropriate behavior to administrators

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Registration Problems

**Issue**: Cannot complete registration
- **Solution**: Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check that you've accepted the terms of service
- Verify your profile image is under 5MB
- Make sure you have a stable internet connection

**Issue**: Account stuck in "Pending" status
- **Solution**: This is normal for new accounts
- An administrator needs to approve your account
- Check back later or contact support

#### Login Issues

**Issue**: Cannot log in automatically
- **Solution**: Ensure you've completed registration
- Check that your device has secure storage enabled
- Try closing and reopening the app
- If persistent, you may need to recover your account with your seed phrase

**Issue**: "Account pending approval" message
- **Solution**: Wait for administrator approval
- This is a one-time process for new accounts
- You'll receive 10 HR Tokens once approved

#### Wallet Issues

**Issue**: Balance not updating
- **Solution**: Pull down to refresh the wallet
- Wait a few moments for blockchain confirmation
- Check your transaction history for pending transactions
- Transactions may take a few minutes to confirm on the blockchain

**Issue**: Transaction stuck in "Pending"
- **Solution**: Blockchain transactions can take time
- Wait 1-5 minutes for confirmation
- Check your internet connection
- If stuck for more than 10 minutes, contact support

#### Contribution Issues

**Issue**: Cannot complete a contribution
- **Solution**: Ensure you haven't already completed it
- Check that you're logged in with an approved account
- Verify you have a stable internet connection
- Try refreshing the contributions list

**Issue**: QR code scan not working
- **Solution**: Grant camera permissions when prompted
- Ensure good lighting for QR code scanning
- Make sure the QR code matches the contribution
- Try scanning from different angles

**Issue**: Share function not working
- **Solution**: Use your device's native share menu
- Ensure the contribution type is "share"
- Check that you haven't already completed the contribution

#### Campaign Issues

**Issue**: Cannot donate to campaign
- **Solution**: Ensure you have sufficient token balance
- Check that the campaign is still active
- Verify your wallet is connected
- Try refreshing the campaign page

**Issue**: Donation transaction fails
- **Solution**: Check your internet connection
- Ensure you have enough tokens (including transaction fees)
- Verify the campaign is still accepting donations
- Try again after a few moments

#### Chat Issues

**Issue**: Messages not sending
- **Solution**: Check your internet connection
- Ensure you're logged in
- Try refreshing the chat
- Close and reopen the chat room

**Issue**: Not receiving messages
- **Solution**: Check your internet connection
- Ensure push notifications are enabled (mobile)
- Refresh the chat list
- Check that you're in the correct chat room

#### General Issues

**Issue**: App crashes or freezes
- **Solution**: Close and reopen the app
- Restart your device
- Update to the latest app version
- Clear app cache (if available)

**Issue**: Slow performance
- **Solution**: Check your internet connection
- Close other apps to free up memory
- Restart the app
- Update to the latest version

### Getting Help

1. **In-App Support**
   - Check the chat channels for community help
   - Contact administrators through chat

2. **Technical Support**
   - Report bugs through the app chat channels
   - Reach out to the development team
   - Check for app updates

3. **Account Recovery**
   - Use your recovery seed phrase
   - Navigate to the Recovery page
   - Enter your seed phrase to restore access

### Security Tips

1. **Protect Your Seed Phrase**
   - Never share it with anyone
   - Store it offline in a secure location
   - Don't take screenshots or photos
   - Write it down on paper

2. **Account Security**
   - Don't share your account credentials
   - Log out on shared devices
   - Report suspicious activity immediately

3. **Transaction Safety**
   - Always verify transaction details before confirming
   - Double-check recipient addresses
   - Be cautious of phishing attempts
