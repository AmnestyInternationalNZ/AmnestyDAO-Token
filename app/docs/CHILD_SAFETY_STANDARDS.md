# Child Safety Standards (Human Rights DAO App)

**Effective date:** 2025-12-23  
**Contact for safety issues:** `sysadmin@matou.nz`

This document describes the Human Rights DAO App’s child safety standards and practices. It is intended to meet app store requirements for apps that include user-generated content and communication features (such as in-app chat).

## Our commitment
We are committed to protecting minors and maintaining a safe environment. We do not allow child sexual exploitation or abuse content, and we take reports seriously.

## Prohibited content and behavior
The following are **strictly prohibited** in the App (including chat):
- **CSAM** (child sexual abuse material) or any sexual content involving minors.
- Sexual solicitation of minors, grooming behavior, or exploitation.
- Content that encourages self-harm, suicide, or violence against minors.
- Harassment, bullying, hate speech, threats, or predatory behavior.
- Sharing personal information of minors (or anyone) without consent, including doxxing.
- Content that facilitates trafficking, abuse, or exploitation.
- Any illegal content involving minors.

## Reporting
If you see content or behavior that violates these standards (especially anything involving minors), report it immediately by emailing `sysadmin@matou.nz` and include:
- what happened and when,
- the chat/channel name (if applicable),
- screenshots or message text (if safe to provide),
- any relevant user identifiers or display names.

## Moderation and enforcement (admin moderated)
The App is **admin moderated**. Admins review reported content and may take actions including:
- removing or restricting access to content or channels,
- restricting chat participation,
- suspending or terminating accounts,
- preserving relevant logs and records for investigation and compliance.

## Response time
We aim to review safety reports as quickly as possible. Reports involving minors, CSAM, or immediate threats are prioritized.

## CSAM handling and escalation
If we become aware of content that may be CSAM or exploitation of minors:
- we will take immediate steps to restrict access to the content and relevant accounts,
- we may preserve evidence as required by law and our legal obligations,
- we may report to appropriate authorities when legally required or appropriate.

## Age appropriateness
The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly allow accounts for children below that age.

## Data and privacy
Safety reporting may involve processing limited user data (e.g., message text and user identifiers) for the purposes of safety, abuse prevention, and compliance. See `docs/PRIVACY_POLICY.md` for details.

## Contact
For child safety concerns, contact `sysadmin@matou.nz`.
