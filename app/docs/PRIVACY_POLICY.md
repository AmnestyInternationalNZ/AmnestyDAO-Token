# Privacy Policy (Human Rights DAO App)

**Effective date:** 2025-12-23  
**Contact:** `sysadmin@matou.nz`

This Privacy Policy explains how the Human Rights DAO App (the “App”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information. The App includes features such as account registration, a wallet (seed phrase), campaign participation/donations, QR code scanning, chat channels, and push notifications.

If anything in this Privacy Policy conflicts with applicable law, applicable law controls.

## What information we collect

### Information you provide
- **Account/profile information**: display name, email address, country, language, areas of interest/affiliations, and (optional) profile image.
- **Chat content**: messages you send in chat channels.
- **Support and safety reports**: information you send to us when you contact support or report content (for example, via email).

### Information the App generates or stores on your device
- **Wallet seed phrase (mnemonic)**: generated on-device and stored in **device secure storage** (Keychain/Keystore via Capacitor secure storage). This is used to access your wallet.  
  - We **do not request** your seed phrase and **you should never share it** with anyone.
- **Authentication token (JWT)** and **user ID**: stored in device secure storage for sign-in.
- **Device identifier (App-generated)**: the App generates a random `device_id` and stores it in local storage for debugging/diagnostics.

### Information collected automatically when you use the App
Depending on your settings and usage, our systems may receive:
- **Logs/diagnostics**: when remote logging is enabled (currently enabled by default), the App sends diagnostic logs to our server, which may include:
  - log message content (which can include error details),
  - timestamp,
  - app version,
  - platform,
  - the App-generated device identifier,
  - and (if you are logged in) your user ID derived from your authentication token.

### Camera / QR codes
If you use QR-code features, the App requests access to your **camera** to scan QR codes. The App uses the camera feed for scanning within the App. We do not intentionally collect or store your camera images as part of QR scanning.

## How we use information
We use information to:
- **Provide the App**: create accounts, authenticate you, show campaigns/contributions, enable chat, and process in-app actions.
- **Operate the wallet features**: generate and use your wallet keys on-device and sign transactions.
- **Process donations and rewards**: build, sign, submit, and confirm blockchain transactions (see “Blockchain and public ledgers”).
- **Communicate with you**: respond to support requests and send updates you request.
- **Safety and integrity**: enforce rules, prevent abuse, and respond to reports.
- **Debugging and security**: monitor errors, troubleshoot issues, and protect the App against misuse.

## Blockchain and public ledgers
The App interacts with the Cardano blockchain. Blockchain transactions and addresses may be public and permanent. This means:
- your **wallet address** and **transaction data** may be visible on public block explorers and to third-party blockchain infrastructure providers,
- we cannot delete or modify blockchain records once published.

## How we share information
We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information:
- **For safety, legal, and compliance reasons**: to respond to lawful requests, protect rights and safety, enforce our terms, and prevent fraud/abuse.

## Data retention
We retain information for as long as needed to provide the App and for legitimate business and legal purposes, including:
- **Account/profile data**: while your account is active and as needed thereafter for compliance and dispute resolution.
- **Chat messages**: while the chat feature is available and for safety/integrity purposes.
- **Logs/diagnostics**: for a limited period necessary for debugging, security, and reliability.

Your **wallet seed phrase** is stored on your device; if you uninstall the App or clear secure storage, you may lose access to your wallet unless you have backed up your seed phrase.

## Security
We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No system is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing your device and **protecting your seed phrase**.

## Your choices and rights
Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to certain processing of your personal information. To request help, contact `sysadmin@matou.nz`.

You may also:
- control camera permission via your device settings,
- stop remote logging by uninstalling the App (and we may add an in-app control in a future update).

## Children’s privacy
The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact `sysadmin@matou.nz`.

## Content moderation (admin moderation)
The App includes chat channels. **Admins moderate reported content** and may take actions such as removing content, restricting chat access, or suspending accounts to enforce safety rules and these policies. If you encounter inappropriate content, you can report it by contacting `sysadmin@matou.nz`.

## International transfers
Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on where our servers and service providers are located.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above when changes are made.
