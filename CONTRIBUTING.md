Contributing to the Human Rights DAO (HRDAO)
Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Human Rights DAO (HRDAO) project.
The HRDAO emerged from a research and development initiative led by Amnesty
International Aotearoa New Zealand exploring participatory governance, civic
engagement, and open-source approaches to collective decision-making.
Contributions are welcome from developers, researchers, designers, human rights
practitioners, community organisers, translators, and others who wish to improve the
project.
